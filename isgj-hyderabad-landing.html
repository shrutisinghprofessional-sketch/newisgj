FAQ

Quick Answers

Who can apply to ISGJ programs?
Most of our diploma, UG and PG programs are open to anyone who has completed 10+2 (12th grade) in any stream. Short-term and certification programs are open to working professionals, jewellery business owners, and design enthusiasts of any background.
Do you offer scholarships?
Yes. ISGJ offers merit-based scholarships of up to ₹50,000 for the 2026 batch, along with EMI & flexible payment plans. Speak to our counsellor to check your eligibility.
What kind of placement support do students get?
Every student receives 100% placement support - including resume building, portfolio reviews, mock interviews, on-campus drives, and direct introductions to India's leading jewellery brands, retailers and export houses.
Where is the Hyderabad campus located?
Our Hyderabad campus is in Jubilee Hills - a premium, accessible location with modern design studios, CAD labs and a jewellery workshop. Visit us for a free campus tour.
Can I do a course alongside a full-time job?
Absolutely. Our short-term and certification programs are designed for working professionals, with weekend & evening batches available.
